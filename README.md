# Truist Bank Business Challenge (2nd Place)

## *Goal*

The financial sector, particularly banking, is increasingly investing in technological advancements to improve customer service and security. Truist, being a significant player, aims to foster trust and security in its technological offerings among its wealth management clients. The goal is to devise a model that predicts and addresses the concerns related to security and trust in mobile banking services, leveraging historical data and NLP techniques.

## *Data*

### Data Preparation

The challenge involves analyzing various datasets, including customer transactions, feedback, and interactions across different platforms to identify patterns and sentiments related to security concerns.


## *Modeling & Model Evaluation*

NLP Techniques: Application of advanced NLP models like BERTopic, VADER, and ROBERTA to analyze text data for sentiment and topic analysis.
  
## *Deployment*

- Operational Integration: The implementation of the model into the banking ecosystem, ensuring seamless integration with existing technological frameworks.
- Cost-Benefit Analysis: Pre-trained Models vs Building from Scratch
